Kariz supports +30 E-commerce marketplace integrations through Mirakl connect. This is the platfom on which many large marketplaces like Bestby, decathlon, mediamarkt and nrodstorm are built on. If one of your customers is on a niche marketplace, there is a high change that it's possible to connect with them through mirakl.

The full list of Mirakl marketplaecs is visible in this tracker. Any seller on any of them can get started with your application by completing the authentication process for mirakl. After you can get the data from all their mirakl marketplaces in the same way you get the data from SHopify.
